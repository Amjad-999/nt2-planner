## English (en-US)

### App name (Title)
```
NT2 Planner — Learn Dutch
```

### Short description
```
Your smart plan to pass the Dutch NT2 exam: vocabulary, grammar & mock exams
```

### Full description
```
NT2 Planner is your complete, free companion for the Dutch NT2 exam, built to take you to the required B1 level — with a fully Arabic interface designed for Arabic speakers learning Dutch.

Instead of juggling dozens of scattered resources, everything you need lives in one place: a daily plan that adapts to your time, practice for all four skills, and DUO-style mock exams.

✨ Why NT2 Planner?

📅 Smart study plan
A flexible daily plan that spreads your tasks up to exam day, with indicators that track your progress and warn you if you fall behind.

📚 Spaced-repetition vocabulary (FSRS)
Learn words with a science-based system that shows each word at the ideal moment to lock it into memory — with flashcards, correct Dutch pronunciation, and fast fuzzy search.

🎧 Official-style mock exams
Practice all four skills — Reading (Lezen), Listening (Luisteren), Writing (Schrijven), Speaking (Spreken) — in DUO exam style, with past practice exams (2023–2025) in audio and text.

📖 Grammar from A1 to B2
Over 40 simplified lessons in Arabic with interactive exercises.

🎯 Interactive exercises
Drag-and-drop sentence ordering, fill-in-the-blank, and word matching.

🗣️ Pronunciation practice
Repeat Dutch sentences and get a score for how close you are.

📊 Analytics & achievements
Charts that track your study minutes and results, plus badges to keep you motivated.

☁️ Optional cloud sync
Sign in to sync progress across devices — merge-only, never deletes. The app works fully without an account.

🔒 Completely free
No ads, no fees, no tracking. Your data stays yours.

📱 Works offline
After the first launch, most of the app works without a connection.

Start your journey to B1 today — one step every day brings you closer to passing.
```
